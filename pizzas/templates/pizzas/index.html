Why Come To Shorty’s?

Shorty’s Pizza Shack is a family owned and operated hangout in the heart of Waco, TX. We’ve been in business since 2011, giving Wacoans a fun, relaxing place to get together, grab a slice, and stick around awhile. Our customers love us for our quality ingredients, like whole milk mozzarella, house-made dough and marinara, from-scratch toppings, fresh salads, and tasty wings. Spend a lazy Sunday afternoon on our giant covered patio with beers by the half-pitcher, catch the game, swing by for a lunch special, or just grab dinner at a spot that the kids love too.
